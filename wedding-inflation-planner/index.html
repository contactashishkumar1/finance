How we calculate Wedding Inflation

Future Cost = Today's Cost × (1 + Inflation)^Years

Lifestyle Creep: We use 10% inflation because premium wedding venues and gold prices historically rise faster than generic consumer goods.

SIP Math: We assume an equity-heavy SIP yielding 12% annually to determine the monthly contribution required.

Why Wedding Inflation is a Financial Challenge

While the Consumer Price Index (CPI) in India often hovers around 5-6%, the Wedding Inflation Index is significantly higher, often touching 10-12%. This is driven by several factors: the increasing cost of real estate for venues, the supply-demand gap during the Saaya (auspicious) dates, and the rising cost of 22K/24K gold which remains the cornerstone of Indian weddings.

Failing to account for these future costs often leads families into "Debt Traps" where high-interest personal loans are used to cover the shortfall. A systematic approach using an SIP (Systematic Investment Plan) specifically for a wedding goal is the most efficient way to maintain your desired lifestyle without diluting your parents' retirement corpus.

Wedding Planning FAQs

What is the average wedding cost in India in 2026?
A typical urban wedding costs ₹25 - ₹40 Lakhs. For higher-income households, destination weddings usually range from ₹75 Lakhs to ₹3 Crores depending on the location and luxury level.
How does 22K gold price impact the budget?
Gold typically accounts for 25% of the Indian wedding budget. Any 10% spike in global gold prices can increase your total wedding expense by nearly 2.5% to 3% instantly.
Is a weekday wedding cheaper in India?
Yes. Opting for a weekday or a "non-Saaya" date can save you up to 30% on venue rentals and decoration costs, as demand is lower compared to weekends.
Can I claim GST input on wedding expenses?
Generally, personal wedding expenses are not eligible for GST input credit under Indian tax laws. Only expenses clearly tied to business promotions or events are deductible.
How much to save monthly for a ₹30 Lakh wedding?
If you have 5 years, a monthly SIP of ₹35,000 at 12% returns will help you reach a ₹30 Lakh goal. Use our calculator above to adjust for inflation.
Is a Wedding Loan a good idea in 2026?
Only as a last resort. Wedding loans are usually high-interest personal loans (11% - 16%). It is far better to start a systematic investment early to avoid starting your marriage with a heavy debt burden.
