RBI’s New LTV Rules 2026 Explained

Starting April 2026, the RBI has introduced a tiered Loan-to-Value (LTV) structure for gold loans to manage household leverage more effectively. For smaller "Emergency" loans under ₹2.5 Lakhs, you can borrow up to 85% of the gold value. For medium loans (₹2.5L to ₹5L), the cap is 80%, and for larger credit needs above ₹5 Lakhs, the standard LTV remains 75%.

This ensures that those with smaller needs get higher liquidity, while large borrowers maintain a healthy equity buffer in case of gold price volatility.

Technical Insight: Repayment Flexibility

Bullet Repayment is a unique feature of Gold Loans. You only pay the interest during the tenure and pay the entire principal at the end. Or, in some schemes, you pay nothing monthly and pay the total (Principal + Interest) at the end of 12 months.

Monthly EMIs are standard for Personal Loans, where every payment covers a portion of both interest and principal. If you are a salaried individual with fixed monthly income, EMIs are safer; if you are a business owner awaiting a payout, Bullet Repayment is better.

Frequently Asked Questions

Is a gold loan cheaper than a personal loan in 2026?
Generally, yes. Since a gold loan is secured by collateral, interest rates range from 8.5% to 12%. Personal loans, being unsecured, range from 11% to 18% depending on your CIBIL score.
Does a gold loan affect my CIBIL score?
Taking a gold loan does not depend on a high CIBIL score, but defaults or late payments are reported to credit bureaus and will negatively impact your score.
What happens if gold prices drop after I take the loan?
If the LTV exceeds the margin due to a price drop, the bank may ask for extra gold or a partial principal repayment (margin call) to maintain the required LTV ratio.
Can I use Gold Bonds (SGB) as collateral?
Yes, Sovereign Gold Bonds (SGB) can be pledged for a loan at most major banks. The LTV and interest rates are usually similar to physical gold loans.
Which is better for a 6-month emergency?
For short-term emergencies, gold loans are better as they have lower processing fees and no prepayment penalties, unlike personal loans which may have lock-in periods.
Are housewives and students eligible?
Yes. Since gold loans are fully secured, banks do not require proof of income, making them accessible to students, housewives, and non-salaried individuals.
What are the documents required for an instant gold loan?
You only need 1. Identity Proof (Aadhaar/PAN), 2. Address Proof, and 3. Passport size photographs. No salary slips or IT returns are needed.
