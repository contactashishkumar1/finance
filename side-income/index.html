How is Side Income Wealth Calculated?

Weekly Hours: Time dedicated per week.

Monthly Gross: Weekly Hours × Hourly Rate × 4 weeks.

Compounding: We apply monthly compounding to the reinvested portion.

Frequently Asked Questions

How is side income taxed in India?
Side income is typically taxed as 'Profits and Gains of Business or Profession.' Professionals can use Section 44ADA to pay tax on only 50% of gross receipts.
Should I reinvest my side income?
Yes, reinvesting side income is the fastest way to accelerate financial independence. Investing ₹10,000 monthly can build massive wealth over time. Compare this with CAGR growth expectations.
