About ArthCalculator

Independent personal finance tools platform focused on practical financial calculations.

Simplifying complex financial decisions through free, transparent, and precise tools.

Our Mission

Financial decisions in India are often driven by emotions or incomplete information. Our mission is to provide simple, easy-to-use financial tools that empower everyday people to understand their money better.

ArthCalculator provides practical tools such as SIP calculators, EMI calculators, tax estimators, and retirement planning tools that help individuals make informed financial decisions.

We believe in complete transparency. ArthCalculator does not provide financial advisory, we do not sell insurance or mutual fund products, and we do not have a hidden agenda. We only provide the data—you make the decision.

Who This Website Is For

Salaried individuals planning monthly savings.

First-time investors exploring mutual funds or FDs.

Loan borrowers calculating EMI and interest impact.

Individuals trying to understand taxes, retirement savings, and long-term wealth creation.

Anyone planning for long-term financial goals.

About the Creator

The founder of this platform is a Qualified Chartered Accountant working in the finance industry with experience in financial planning and analysis (FP&A). He built ArthCalculator to simplify real financial formulas such as compound interest, loan amortization, tax calculations, and retirement corpus projections.

His professional background in data-driven financial planning shapes the design of these tools. ArthCalculator was developed as an independent educational platform focused on simplifying complex financial calculations into practical, real-world applications that help individuals save, invest, and borrow more confidently.
