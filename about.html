About Financial Suite India

Independent personal finance tools platform focused on practical financial calculations.

Simplifying complex financial decisions through free, transparent, and precise tools.

Our Mission

Financial decisions in India are often driven by emotions or incomplete information. Our mission is to provide **simple, easy-to-use financial tools** that empower everyday people to understand their money better.

We believe in complete transparency. Financial Suite India does not provide financial advisory, we do not sell insurance or mutual fund products, and we do not have a hidden agenda. We only provide the data—you make the decision.

Who This Website Is For

Salaried individuals planning monthly savings.

First-time investors exploring mutual funds or FDs.

Loan borrowers calculating EMI and interest impact.

Anyone planning for long-term financial goals.

About the Creator

**Ashish Kumar** is a Chartered Accountant working in the finance industry with experience in **financial planning and analysis (FP&A)**. He has created this platform to provide simple financial calculators that help individuals understand investments, loans, and savings decisions.

His professional background in data-driven financial planning shapes the design of these tools. Financial Suite India was developed as an independent educational platform focused on simplifying complex financial calculations into practical, real-world applications that help individuals save, invest, and borrow more confidently.

What This Website Does NOT Do

No product selling or brokerage services.

No affiliate-based investment advice or hidden referrals.

No individual portfolio recommendations or financial planning services.
