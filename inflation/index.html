What is Inflation?

Inflation is the rate at which the general level of prices for goods and services is rising. As inflation grows, every rupee you own buys a smaller percentage of a good or service. This process is essentially the erosion of purchasing power.

Why Inflation Matters for Financial Planning

Failing to account for inflation is a critical mistake. If you save ₹1 Crore for retirement today, its value will be vastly different in 20 years. Plan smarter with tools like our SIP Calculator or SWP Calculator.
