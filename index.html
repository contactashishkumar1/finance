Investment Strategy: Fixed vs Market Growth

Safe Assets (PPF/FD): Conservative tools like the Public Provident Fund are essential for capital protection. They offer government-backed safety and tax-free returns, which is vital for long-term pillars like daughter's education or pension.

Growth Assets (SIP): To truly build wealth and beat the cost of living, equity-linked SIPs are unmatched. Historically, the Indian Nifty 50 has provided a CAGR of 12-14% over decades. Use our SIP tool to model different market return scenarios.

Balancing Debt: While investing is important, debt management is crucial. If your Home Loan EMI interest rate is higher than your post-tax FD rate, consider making principal prepayments to save on the total interest burden.
