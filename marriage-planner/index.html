Navigating Wedding Costs in 2026 India

The "Big Fat Indian Wedding" is not just a cultural phenomenon but a major financial milestone for most households. In 2026, the wedding industry in India continues to grow, with costs for venues, catering, and photography rising faster than general consumer inflation. Using a Marriage Planning Calculator is the only way to ensure that your dream celebration doesn't compromise your retirement or other long-term goals.

Planning for a wedding involves two distinct phases: 1) Determining the Future Value of your current budget and 2) Calculating the SIP (Systematic Investment Plan) required to build that corpus. By starting early, you let time do the heavy lifting through compounding, allowing you to reach high targets with relatively small monthly contributions.

The "Double Inflation" Factor

When planning for a marriage, you must account for what financial experts call "lifestyle inflation." This isn't just about the rising price of gold or food; it's about the rising standards of celebrations. A "standard" wedding 10 years ago involved a local hall; today, it involves multi-day events and professional event planners. Our calculator allows you to set a higher inflation rate (10-12%) to account for these shifts in expectations.

Wedding Planning Frequently Asked Questions

1. Should I take a Personal Loan for a wedding?
Mathematically, no. Personal loans for weddings often carry high interest rates (11-16% p.a.). It is much more efficient to save in advance via SIPs, where you earn interest instead of paying it. Use a loan only for small gaps in your budget.
2. How to manage taxes on the wedding fund?
If you are using equity mutual funds, capital gains above ₹1.25 Lakh per year are taxed at 12.5% (LTCG). Always target a corpus that is 10% higher than your actual requirement to account for taxes upon withdrawal.
3. Can I use Sukanya Samriddhi Yojana (SSY) for marriage?
Yes. SSY allows withdrawal of the entire balance for the marriage of the girl child once she attains 18 years of age. It is a highly tax-efficient and secure way to build a marriage fund.
4. What is the impact of guest list size on budget?
In 2026, catering and venue are the largest variable costs. Every 50 additional guests can increase your budget by ₹1 Lakh to ₹3 Lakhs depending on the city. Managing the guest list is the fastest way to control costs.
