Frequently Asked Questions

1. How much does a standard wedding cost in India in 2026?
A middle-class wedding in 2026 typically ranges between ₹15 Lakhs to ₹30 Lakhs, while premium or destination weddings can easily exceed ₹50 Lakhs to ₹1 Crore.
2. What inflation rate should I use for wedding planning?
Lifestyle and wedding services often experience higher inflation than the general CPI. A safe assumption is 10% to 12% annual inflation for wedding costs in India.
3. Should I take a Personal Loan for a wedding?
Mathematically, no. Personal loans for weddings often carry high interest rates (11-16% p.a.). It is much more efficient to save in advance via SIPs, where you earn interest instead of paying it.
4. How to manage taxes on the wedding fund?
If you are using equity mutual funds, capital gains above ₹1.25 Lakh per year are taxed at 12.5%. Always target a corpus that is 10% higher than your actual requirement to account for taxes.
